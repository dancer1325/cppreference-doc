`std::experimental::packaged_task` may refer to: 

  * [`std::experimental::fundamentals_v1::packaged_task` or `std::experimental::fundamentals_v2::packaged_task`](lib_extensions/packaged_task.html "cpp/experimental/lib extensions/packaged task"), a modified version of [std::packaged_task](../thread/packaged_task.html "cpp/thread/packaged task") provided by the [library fundamentals TS](memory.html "cpp/experimental/lib extensions") with support for type-erased allocators; 
  * [`std::experimental::concurrency_v1::packaged_task`](concurrency/packaged_task.html "cpp/experimental/concurrency/packaged task"), a modified version of `std::packaged_task` provided by the [concurrency TS](concurrency.html "cpp/experimental/concurrency") whose `get_future()` member function returns the improved [`std::experimental::future`](future.html "cpp/experimental/future") provided by that TS.
