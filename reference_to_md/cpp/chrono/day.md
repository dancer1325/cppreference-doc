The class `day` represents a day in a month. Its normal range is `[`1`, `31`]`, but it may hold any number in `[`​0​`, `255`]`. 

`day` is a [TriviallyCopyable](../named_req/TriviallyCopyable.html "cpp/named req/TriviallyCopyable") [StandardLayoutType](../named_req/StandardLayoutType.html "cpp/named req/StandardLayoutType").
