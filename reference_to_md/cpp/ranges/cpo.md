### Explanation

The name `cpo` denotes a _customization point object_ , which is a const [function object](../named_req/FunctionObject.html "cpp/named req/FunctionObject") of a [literal](../named_req/LiteralType.html "cpp/named req/LiteralType") [`semiregular`](../concepts/semiregular.html "cpp/concepts/semiregular") class type. See [CustomizationPointObject](../named_req/CustomizationPointObject.html "cpp/named req/CustomizationPointObject") for details.
